1. Use screwdriver to remove plate over USB outlet.

2. Change printer over to USB mode:
   * Hold down Feed button.
   * Hold down FB again to get to menu.
   * Press FB 17 times (Interface selection), then hold FB.
   * Press FB 2 times (USB), then hold FB. You should see confirmation.
   * Restart printer.

3. Download APD_501_T20II. Run installer.

4. Download TMVirtualPortDriver830b. Run application.
   * Highlight a port (ex: COM1).
   * Click "Assign Port" button at the bottom of the window.
   * The printers plugged in should show up in the menu (ex: ESDPRT001). Highlight it and select "OK".
   * Repeat for each printer.
